How many meters does one Minecraft block equal?

Each block is one meter

 When you first spawn in the game what dimension do you start in?

The overworld. This is where most people spend most of their time in Minecraft.
What is the most famous evil mob in Minecraft?

The creeper of course! This character is iconic in Minecraft and will hiss at you before blowing up next to you.

 What type of food do cows and sheep eat?

Wheat which you can farm by planting seeds.
Where can you find the Drowned Mob?

In dark water. You usually find them in the deep oceans but you can find them underground too. You can also turn zombies into drowned by drowning them in water!

 What is the most common crafting material used

Wood! You need it for so many things in Minecraft.
How many alternative dimensions can you visit as well as the overworld?

The nether which is a world full of lava and scary monsters and The End which is a barren dimension with lots of islands and a very long fall into the void.

 If you kill a zombie what items could you get?

Most commonly zombies drop rotten flesh but they can sometimes drop carrots and potatoes. Rarely a zombie will drop armor, tools and even an iron ingot.
Where can you get enchanted books from?

You can get enchanted books from loot chests or form trading with librarian villagers.

 What tool do you need to gather stone and ore?

Pickaxe. You can craft them from wood, iron, gold, diamond or netherite. Although, not all pickaxes will work for all types of materials.
What tool do you need to make crop farms?

a hoe. These are used to make farmland to plant you crops in. You can also use a hoe to get leaf blocks too.

 Do you know how many versions of Minecraft there are?
